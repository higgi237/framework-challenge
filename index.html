En Pointe Ballet Academy About Us

En Pointe Ballet Academy was founded in 1995 by owner and head instructor Ella French. Ella began ballet lessons when she was four years old and went on to spend just over seven years with the New York City Ballet. Nothing brings her more joy than sharing her passion for dance with others.

Originally located in New York City, En Pointe Ballet Academy has expanded over the years, and is now operating in the following locations:

Seattle, Washington

Our newest location and most modern location, with an opening date of May 15th, 2023.

Chicago, Illinois

Located minutes from the infamous Chicago bean, our Illinois location features incredibly city views.

Boston, Massachusetts

Sitting just outside the bustling city of Boston, this location features beautiful architecture.
